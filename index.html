Our Process

The science of
restoration

01

Clean

Debris Removal

We start by blowing off all leaves and debris with a leaf blower, power wash with degreaser then scrubbing the entire deck surface with a stiff brush to remove dirt and buildup, ensuring a clean, ready foundation.

02

Sand

Surface Preparation

We use professional-grade orbital sanders to strip away years of weathering and grey wood fibers, revealing fresh open grain. Every board is sanded to 80-120 grit for optimal stain absorption.

03

Stain

Protective Finish

Premium, oil-based stains are hand-applied with precision brushwork. Each coat bonds deep into the wood fibers, delivering rich colour along with UV protection and water resistance built for Ottawa's harsh winters.
